01

Overview

HomeVault provides tools for organizing home inventory, receipts, warranties, maintenance records, property details, and insurance-related documentation.

By using HomeVault, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Important: HomeVault does not sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.
02

Data We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, phone number, and login credentials
  • Inventory information you create, including item details, purchase records, receipts, and photos
  • Property, insurance, warranty, maintenance, and claims information you choose to store
  • Device and usage data needed to improve reliability and security
03

How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and secure your HomeVault account
  • To store and organize your home and property records
  • To provide reminders for warranties, maintenance, and renewal events
  • To support receipt scanning, barcode lookup, and related app features
  • To improve the app, diagnose problems, and respond to support requests
04

Storage & Security

We use industry-standard administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, including secure transport, access controls, and credential protection.

No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we work to protect your data appropriately for the service we provide.

05

Third-Party Services

HomeVault may rely on third-party infrastructure and service providers for hosting, storage, email delivery, authentication, AI-assisted features, and similar functions.

Those providers receive only the data needed to perform the specific service for HomeVault.

06

Your Rights

  • Request access to the personal data associated with your account
  • Request correction of inaccurate account information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal or operational obligations
  • Contact us with privacy questions or concerns
07

Retention

We keep your information for as long as necessary to provide the HomeVault service or comply with legal, security, and operational requirements.
